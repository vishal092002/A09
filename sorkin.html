Andrew Ross Sorkin, author of Too Big to Fail

November 2015

New York times columnist and author, Andrew Ross Sorken has been described as the most famous journalist of his generation.
His book, too big to fail, was the first behind the scenes account of the current recession.

The Economist, The financial Times, and Business Week, all named to Big To Fail, one of the best books of the year. The book was Published by Viking october 20, 2009. The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Filims and premiered on HBO May 23 2011.
